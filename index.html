Getting Closer,Safer

These simple tipscan help make your best times together also the safest!

  • Wear UV protective sunglasses and sunscreen with SPF15 or higher.
  • Keep yourself hydrated at all times
  • If you are accompained by children,make sure that they know how to contact you.
  • Maintain discipline in the ride queue area
  • Pools are shallow.DO NOT dive
  • Pregnant women,heart patients,people who have undergone surgeries in the recent past, people with high blood pressure and those prone to epilespsy are adviced to avoid fast/high-thrill rides
  • Wear nylon or synthetic clothes inside the park.Do not wear loose garments like sarees,dupattas,burqas,etc for your own safety.
  • Please follow safety instructions of lifeguards,ride operators and instructions dislayed near the rides.


    • Dress Up for Family Funvilla

    To ensure that the water used in our various water rides is clean and hygienc,we enforce a strict dress code.

  • Always wear apparel made of Nylon or 100% synthetic material(t-shirt,shorts,bermudas,three-fourths or swim suits)
  • Sarees,churidars/salwar,dupatta,formal pants,shirts,burkhas/purdahs,school uniforms,denims(jeans and three-fourth), cargos etc.are not permitted in the water rides.Almost all our rides involve motion/moving parts.Thats's why we request our guests to refrain from wearing long or loose garments like Saree/Dupatta/Shawls/Scarves/Hijab/Mafta/Hats etc.in any of the rides,as it could lead to potential harm to themselves or others.
  • In case you are not wearing or carrying garments that fit the bill,You could purchase Nylon/Synthetic clothes (A pair of T-shirts & Shorts start at INR 150.)from the stores in the park near the changing room/locker area at a nominal price.
