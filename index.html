Getting Closer,Safer

These simple tipscan help make your best times together also the safest!

  • Wear UV protective sunglasses and sunscreen with SPF15 or higher.
  • Keep yourself hydrated at all times
  • If you are accompained by children,make sure that they know how to contact you.
  • Maintain discipline in the ride queue area
  • Pools are shallow.DO NOT dive
  • Pregnant women,heart patients,people who have undergone surgeries in the recent past, people with high blood pressure and those prone to epilespsy are adviced to avoid fast/high-thrill rides
  • Wear nylon or synthetic clothes inside the park.Do not wear loose garments like sarees,dupattas,burqas,etc for your own safety.
  • Please follow safety instructions of lifeguards,ride operators and instructions dislayed near the rides.
